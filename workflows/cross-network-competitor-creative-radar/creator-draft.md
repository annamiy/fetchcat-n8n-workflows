# Creator draft: Cross-Network Competitor Creative Radar

**Title:** Build a cross-network competitor creative radar for Meta, Google, and TikTok

**Summary:** Generate a weekly evidence-linked Markdown brief from public Meta, Google, and TikTok ad-library data without inventing a cross-platform performance score.

**Template value:** Helps marketers and agencies monitor a narrow competitor watchlist, compare current and prior public creative snapshots, and turn observed hooks/offers/proof points into original test hypotheses.

**Required credentials:** Apify and Google Drive. Optional: OpenAI-compatible model and Slack.

**Limitations:** Public-source coverage only. Signals differ by network and are not comparable performance metrics. TikTok Creative Center Top Ads is a curated surface, not a complete TikTok ad archive.
